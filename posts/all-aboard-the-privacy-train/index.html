All aboard the privacy train!

Jan 17, 2021
2 min read
privacy gafam

For the last couple of years I have been on a mission to remove the big 5 tech companies from my life, Google, Apple, Facebook, Amazon and Microsoft, or GAFAM for short. I have to admit now that his has been a total failure thus far, however for 2021 I decided to make some progress on this endevour.

I will state the obvious now, if you want privacy, dont have your last name as your domain but the damage there is done. This venture is about limiting the amount of personal data I share with these companies not becoming a hermit and falling off the face of the earth.

So, I started out the year:

  • Moving my devices to using NextDNS and block known tracker domains
  • Deleting Facebook/Messenger/Instagram/WhatsApp and adding the no-facebook blocklist to NextDNS.
  • Deleting all Google accounts.
  • Moved my email hosting over to FastMail (one of the easiest email migrations I have ever done üëè)
  • Deleting LinkedIn (again). (Urggg recruiter spam)
  • Migrate all my various stuff from AWS to Microsoft Azure.
  • Cancelling Amazon Prime and deleting my Amazon account.

I moved from using a Google Pixel device back to an Apple iPhone. This is all about what I consider to be the lesser of two evils, my personal view here is that Apple isnt motivated by building huge user profiles in order to sell more ads, or at least, such motivation would be less than that of Google.

The only “social media profile” I still have is my GitHub account but I figure thats ok as its owned by Microsoft and I personally use Azure for all my hosting.

You will also see there are no trackers on this site! No analytics, No fingerprinting.

My next task is moving over to a decentralised messaging platform like Matrix, just have to convince the rest of the family on that one!
